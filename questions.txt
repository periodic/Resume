# Questions for Interviews

## Development Practices

* What development process do you use?  Agile? Waterfall?

* How would you define your design process, TDD, BDD, monolithic specification, or other?

* What souce-code management system do you use?

* How do you determine if an error/bug/outage is caused by a systematic failure, and how do you determine the root cause?

## Human Resources

* What training and learning to you employ to ensure your team's skills stay up to date?

* How do you measure employee performance?

* If the project requires extra work to avoid slipping, how do you motivate the team to put in the extra effort and time to finish on time?

* What do you guys do for fun?
